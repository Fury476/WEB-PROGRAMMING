Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Simple, creamy, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Cook the spaghetti in boiling salted water until al dente. Drain and save a little of the water.
  2. Fry pancetta in olive oil until crispy. Toss in the garlic for a quick sauté.
  3. Whisk eggs, Parmesan, and pepper together in a bowl.
  4. Mix the pasta with the pancetta (take the pan off the heat first!)
  5. Quickly stir in the egg mix — use a splash of pasta water to make it nice and creamy.
  6. Plate it up and sprinkle on extra Parmesan and black pepper. Yum!
