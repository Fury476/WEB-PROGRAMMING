Stranger Things

Plot Overview

Set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, *Stranger Things* follows the disappearance of a young boy, Will Byers, and the supernatural events that unfold as his friends, family, and the local police search for him. A girl with telekinetic powers, known as Eleven, escapes a secret government lab and joins Will's friends—Mike, Dustin, and Lucas—to uncover a sinister parallel dimension called the Upside Down. The series blends horror, sci-fi, and nostalgia as it explores friendship, bravery, and the fight against otherworldly threats.

Key Information

Created by: The Duffer Brothers

First Aired: July 15, 2016

Network: Netflix

Genre: Sci-Fi, Horror, Drama

Seasons: 4 (as of March 2025, Season 5 in production)

Cast & Creative Team

Creators: Matt Duffer, Ross Duffer

Main Cast:

  • Winona Ryder as Joyce Byers
  • David Harbour as Jim Hopper
  • Millie Bobby Brown as Eleven
  • Finn Wolfhard as Mike Wheeler
  • Gaten Matarazzo as Dustin Henderson
  • Caleb McLaughlin as Lucas Sinclair

Key Characters

Eleven: A mysterious girl with psychokinetic abilities.

Mike Wheeler: The leader of the friend group, loyal and brave.

Jim Hopper: Hawkins' gruff police chief with a tragic past.

Joyce Byers: Will’s determined and fierce mother.

Dustin Henderson: The quirky, science-loving member of the gang.
